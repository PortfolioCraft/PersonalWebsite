Business Profile

In 2013, she founded Gain Group of Companies, a conglomerate with diverse business interests across various industries. Under her leadership, the company has flourished and solidified its position as a key player in the business landscape. Gain Group includes Gain Healthcare, Gain Business Support, Gain Logistics, and Gain Social Housing, offering employment to more than 200 employees with offices based in the UK, South Africa, and Zimbabwe. Registered with the Institute of Directors in both the UK and Zimbabwe, Dr Rudo recently expanded her business operations to South Africa. 

Qualifications

In addition to her successful business ventures and philanthropic endeavours, Dr Rudo holds an LLB degree which was conferred to her in October 2023 from The Open University, she is also currently completing her LLM. Being the Director of Gain Group of Companies she also serves as the CQC Nominated Individual for Gain Healthcare. With a Level 5 qualification in care provision, she oversees the company’s services for individuals with complex needs .

Recognition

She has been invited to speak at various platforms worldwide in functions including The Female Founders Initiative Middle East and Africa due to her business acumen and philanthropic works. She has also earned recognition with organisations including as The Women of Wisdom where she was honoured for her work uplifting women and children in 2019, Zim Achievers awards where she was honoured with an award as the UK female personality for the 2019 in recognition of her charity works, Community Champion award which was conferred to her in 2021.
